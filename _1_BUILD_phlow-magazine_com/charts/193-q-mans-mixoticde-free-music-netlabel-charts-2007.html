Q-Man's/ Mixotic free music netlabel charts 2007

Mixotic is a netlabel publishing weekly free DJ sets, focused on electronic music in the broadest sense. Q-Man is the head behind this label. Under this moniker he’s mixing electronic club music with a certain amount of rave appeal. His second pseudonym Entactogen stands for a more chilled and emotional style, made out of downbeats, indietronica and vocal tracks.
